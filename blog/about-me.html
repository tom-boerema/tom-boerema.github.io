About Me

I live half the time in Margaret River, Western Australia and half the time in Zurich, Switzerland (I'm living there at the moment). It's a nice balance of country and city life. I don't really have any experience in programming except for some basic C++ at Uni. I found it very boring as the application was not useful. However, now I am motivated to learn as I have a useful application for the skillset.

I studied mechanical engineering and worked in oil & gas for a few years but found it unfullfilling, so I decided to try and pursue some more entrepenerial ventures and music.

It took a lot of trial and error to find out what I wanted to do - I even worked as a shepherd in the South Island for a while - but I am happy where I am and am greatful for that.

I still wonder if it is better to be an expert in one thing or be average at many things, I am definitely the latter and now I'm adding web development to the list! I believe focus important but on the other hand, having working knowledge in many domains gives you perspective to make good decisions.
